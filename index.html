Beautiful keepsakes for your family

My name is Diana, and I am the creator of Morlee Makes, a small business selling hand-crafted keepsakes that are designed to celebrate and honor your loved ones.

I trained as a graphic designer before marrying my husband and becoming a mother to two beautiful children. I later discovered a love for caligraphy, which I now feature in many of my products.

You will find me at the Morrinsville markets on the first Saturday of every month.

What can I create for you?

To make an order, simply fill out the form below with your request. Make sure to include the specific details you would like for any custom orders, such as the correct spelling of names or the particular colours you would like on your rainbow.

After you submit your order, I will be in contact with you within 3 working days with payment instructions. I will also double check with you that I have correctly understood your instructions regarding custom details.

Once payment has cleared, I will begin crafting your item. This will typically take 2 weeks to complete, at which point you will be able to collect it or pay for delivery.
